Chapter One

When S meet A

2020

First Glances at Tumsar ✨

Our story began amidst the celebration and joy of a family wedding in Tumsar. In the middle of the crowd, our paths crossed, and a quiet, beautiful spark was born. I was in Nagpur and you were in Bhandara, but even the distance between our cities couldn't fade the memory of that special night.

Nov 16, 2022

A Simple "Hey" on Snapchat 💬

Two years later, fate quietly brought us back. We connected on Snapchat, and our very first chat started with a shy 'hey' and a simple 'hello'. Those small messages soon became a bridge of laughter and notes, bringing Bhandara and Nagpur closer every single day.

Late 2022

Whispers of a Heartbeat ❤️

As our conversations grew longer, our feelings grew deeper. The day came when we finally confessed what was in our hearts: I told you that I loved you, and you shyly admitted that you had a crush on me. But love is a journey with hills to climb; due to outside circumstances and reasons, we faced a painful breakup the very next day.

2025

Reconnecting the Threads 💫

Years passed, but the unspoken bond remained. In 2025, you reached out and said, 'I want you back.' My heart didn't hesitate for a single second—I replied, 'I love you too,' and just like that, we picked up the threads of our story.

May 2025

Nagpur Reunion & Our Promise 🌸

A few days into our reconnection, fears got the better of me, and I suggested we part ways again. You asked, 'but why?', holding onto the bond we shared. Everything shifted in May when you moved to Nagpur for your NID and UCEED classes, living in the hostel. Seeing you in person changed everything. I looked into your eyes and said, 'I love you, let's make this real.' And you replied, 'I love you too.'

First Date

Meeting in the Real World 👩‍❤️‍👨

I met you in person for the first time in Nagpur, alongside Riya. Transitioning our late-night chats and calls into the warmth of the real world was magical—laughing, sharing stories, and realizing how real this connection truly is.

The Birthday surprise

An Unexpected Gift 🎁

I planned your birthday with the most exciting gift—something you truly didn't expect from me. Seeing the pure joy and disbelief on your face is a memory I will carry with me forever.

Simple Joys

Scooty Rides & Lake Sunsets 🛵

Our days were filled with simple, happy memories. Endless scooty rides with the wind in our faces, Pani Puri challenges, and sharing Dabeli. Your favorite 'Sadha Soda' was always a must-have, and I loved watching you enjoy every South Indian dish. We spent peaceful afternoons listening to the water at Sonegaon Lake and watching sunsets at Zilpi Lake.

Our Travels

From Nagpur Temples to Ujjain ⛩️

Our first trip together was visiting 4 to 5 temples across Nagpur, seeking blessings for our future. But our absolute favorite memory is our trip to Ujjain. The journey, the prayers to Mahakal, and being side-by-side made us closer and stronger than we had ever been.

Today

A Promise for Our Future 🔐

Everything was going so beautifully, and I don't know whose shadow or curse fell upon us to bring us to this difficult day. Anushka, you told me about a breakup, but that is a massive decision for us. I don't want to lose what we've built. Please take your time, look at everything we share, and look deep into your heart. I promise my love for you will never fade—and I hope yours won't either.
